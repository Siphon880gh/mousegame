Mouse Engine API Example: General

Open DevTools console, then perform different mouse clicks on this webpage to see the output.

Expected behaviors:

  • Single left click will console log "LMB tapped" but there's a slight delay before that will output.
  • Single right click will console log "RMB tapped"
  • Left click followed by right click within 350ms will console log "LMB then RMB combo detected".
